# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Everyone who participates in this project — contributors, maintainers, and users — deserves a welcoming and respectful experience. We pledge to make participation in this project and its community a positive experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our standards

**Behavior we encourage:**

- Being kind and respectful in all communications
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback graciously
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community
- Assuming good faith in others' contributions and questions

**Behavior that is not acceptable:**

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Deliberately hostile or dismissive responses
- Sustained disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions
- Any form of harassment or unwelcome contact

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to the project maintainer at [shakibihamidreza@gmail.com](mailto:shakibihamidreza@gmail.com). All reports are reviewed confidentially.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with these standards, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation for persistent violations.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other venue where the project is represented.
